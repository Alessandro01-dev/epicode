About

This project is a curated exploration of the music that has left a lasting influence on me. It brings together a selection of my favorite albums, primarily from the golden decades of the 1970s and 1980s, when rock and its many variations reached new creative heights.

These albums represent more than just records: they are milestones in modern music history. Each one showcases the artistry, innovation, and storytelling that defined an era and continues to inspire listeners today.

Rather than a definitive list, this site is a personal journey through the sounds that continue to resonate with me. It is a space to rediscover timeless works, reflect on their impact, and celebrate the enduring power of music to connect, inspire, and move us.
